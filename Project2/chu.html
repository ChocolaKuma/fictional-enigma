Chobits

Ah Chobits. No matter how many times I
read this it never gets old. I've read every Different English
translation of this that is out there. Here is a short synopsis about
what this one is about it:

Hideki's just like every other guy
in the 22nd century. He just wants a good job, a good car, and a
sexy robot girlfriend to call his own. Until he lands a job, he'll
never be able to afford his own 'Persocom' companion. Hideki's
luck changes when he discovers Chi--an adorable but seemingly stupid
Persocom--tied up in a pile of trash. His first robot companion
turns out to be a lot more responsibility than he expected, and
she gets him into quite a few embarrassing situations. It's
'boy-meets-girl' for the cyber age. (Tokyopop)

So then lets get crack-en on with the
review. Over all I really enjoyed this one. like I've said earlier
I have read this just a few times (closer to 20 times). This was
my first Ecchi-Android-Romance manga/anime of all time. For those
who are wondering the manga is way better. Chi is probably the
reason why I went into computers in the first place. It was the Idea
of a computer like a Persocom that blew my mind. After
reading this I used a mix of programs to give my computer an AI
like system and got it to talk to me (god I'm forever alone
*insert face palm here*). This manga really touched me. The first time
I read it I cried at the end because of such a good ending. A side
note I made what sumomo says when she gets a call into a ringtone.
I love it.

Quilty of Translation 9/10
Story 10/10
Art  9/10
Concept 10/10

Over all 10/10 (Its a must read)
